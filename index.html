About

I'm an immersive XR designer — I take games and experiences from concept to delivery, and I like the unglamorous part of the craft: laying out a space, pacing its encounters, and rebuilding it from playtest feedback. I set design pillars early, prototype fast, and iterate hard.

On Shatterfall, a tabletop game built with a team of four, I worked on the map layouts, enemy spawning, and difficulty across two full external playtest cycles. I took a lead/production role on a VR helmsman trainer adopted by the Royal Navy, recreating a ship's bridge as a believable, learnable space. I've also shipped XR for Take-Two Interactive (Apple Vision Pro) and the award-winning E1 Series, and graduated with First-Class Honours and an Outstanding Student award.

I'm a lifelong RPG and FPS player — D&D, Subnautica and BG3-style systems are the vocabulary I think in — and my next step is moving into level and game design. I'm comfortable across the pipeline: Unreal, Unity, Blender, Figma, and motion-capture stages.
