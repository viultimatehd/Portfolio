Overview

The E1 Series is the world's first electric powerboat racing championship. Badass Studios was brought in to make the racing more legible and exciting for a broadcast audience through augmented reality. I joined the project to help take it from concept through to on-site delivery.

The headline idea — which I was responsible for conceiving and pitching — was to capture live data from the championship and build a real-time digital twin of the race, so viewers could see positions, gaps, and track information rendered as AR over the broadcast feed.

What I did

  • Owned the concept and outreach for the real-time digital-twin project, including the pitch for Epic Games' MegaGrant programme.
  • Designed AR applications on rapid timelines with real-time data integrations feeding live race telemetry.
  • Helped design the E1 Series mobile app, including the user interface for the companion experience.
  • Ran quality assurance across AR broadcast, digital-twin, and simulator builds.
  • Handled on-site setup and operation of AR broadcast elements and simulators during live events.
  • Produced asset breakdowns and supported project budgeting.
