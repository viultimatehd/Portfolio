Overview

The E1 Series is the world's first electric powerboat racing championship. Badass Studios was brought in to make the racing more legible and exciting for a broadcast audience through augmented reality. I joined the project to help take it from concept through to on-site delivery.

The headline idea was a real-time digital twin of the race — capturing live data so viewers could see positions, gaps, and track information rendered as AR over the broadcast feed. I contributed to that concept and its outreach, and I owned the AR app that brought the experience to audiences — making every design decision from UI and UX through to scene composition.

What I did

  • Owned the E1 AR app end to end — led development and made every decision from UI and UX through to scene composition.
  • Integrated live race telemetry into the AR experience on rapid delivery timelines.
  • Contributed to the concept and outreach for the real-time digital-twin project, including support for the Epic Games MegaGrant pitch.
  • Ran quality assurance across AR broadcast, digital-twin, and simulator builds.
  • Handled on-site setup and operation of AR broadcast elements and simulators during live events.
  • Produced asset breakdowns and supported project budgeting.
