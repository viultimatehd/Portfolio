Overview

Alongside the AR work, Badass Studios built a racing simulator game for the E1 Series — a way for fans to get behind the wheel of the electric powerboats, both online and at live race weekends. The sizzle above is from the Miami round.

I worked on the simulator as a tester and supporting developer, and ran the arcade gaming setup at live events so fans could play on-site.

What I did

  • Tested and supported development of the simulator across builds, feeding issues and feedback back into the team.
  • Ran arcade gaming support at live events — setup, operation and front-of-house for fans.
  • Helped tune the experience for a fan-engagement audience: quick to pick up, fun to play, true to the racing.
